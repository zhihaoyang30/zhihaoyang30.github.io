Co-authored Papers
FlowPIE: Test-Time Scientific Idea Evolution with Flow-Guided Literature Exploration
Qiyao Wang, Hongbo Wang, Longze Chen, Zhihao Yang, Guhong Chen, Hamid Alinejad-Rokny, Hui Li, Yuan Lin, Min Yang.
